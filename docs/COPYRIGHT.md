# Copyright, Attribution, Asset Rights, and Takedown Policy

This repository publishes prompts, structured metadata, and example outputs. It
does not grant rights in third-party brands, packaging, source photographs,
artwork, characters, people, or other protected material.

## Attribution is not permission

Naming an author or linking to Pinterest does not by itself authorize copying,
hosting, sublicensing, or commercial reuse. Public availability and popularity
also do not establish a compatible license.

The repository may redistribute an asset only when at least one of these bases is
documented:

- the repository or contributor created it and grants the stated license;
- the rights holder granted compatible permission;
- an identified license permits the intended redistribution;
- the work is reliably documented as public-domain.

“Publish now and remove later” is not an accepted permission basis.

## Repository-authored material

Unless a record states otherwise, repository-authored prompt text,
documentation, translations, and generated preview images are licensed under
CC BY 4.0.

When reusing them:

1. credit the named author or “CommercePrompt Atlas contributors”;
2. link to this repository or record;
3. identify CC BY 4.0;
4. state whether the material was modified.

## Community and licensed material

Each record separately identifies:

- author and original publication;
- source type;
- permission basis;
- content or asset license;
- visual-research and license-evidence links;
- review status.

The repository license applies only to rights the contributor was entitled to
grant. It does not erase third-party rights.

## Pinterest and other discovery services

Pinterest links may be recorded as `visual-research` references. The project may
study non-exclusive ideas such as lighting direction, camera angle, background
class, composition, color relationship, and merchandising format, then author a
new prompt and generate a new example.

Pinterest-hosted files and images discovered through Pinterest must not be
committed as previews unless the underlying creator and a compatible permission
basis are independently verified. The validator rejects Pinterest-hosted preview
URLs while allowing Pinterest research links.

## Brands, people, and product claims

Platform and brand names may appear descriptively to identify a workflow. Their
owners do not sponsor or endorse this repository. Contributors must not submit:

- counterfeit or deceptive listing material;
- private or non-consensual likenesses;
- unlicensed copyrighted characters or artwork;
- invented ratings, certifications, reviews, specifications, or performance claims;
- misleading product Before/After results.

## Reporting a problem

Use the **Copyright or rights report** Issue form to request removal, corrected
attribution, corrected license metadata, or review of copyright, trademark,
privacy, publicity, or product-claim concerns.

Include the prompt ID or repository URL, your relationship to the work, the
requested resolution, and non-sensitive evidence. Do not publish identity
documents, private addresses, contracts, or other sensitive evidence in a public
Issue.

## Review and removal

Maintainers may mark disputed content `removed` while reviewing a report and may
remove material whenever its permission basis, provenance, safety, or accuracy
cannot be supported. Corrections and substantiated removal requests should be
handled promptly.

This operational policy is not legal advice.
